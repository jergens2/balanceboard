Instructions

Your rotation is the primary repeating cycle in your life.
Almost everyone spends their time in an arrangement which is rotational. For most people, this usually means arranging your time around your scheduled work or school hours.
In most cases, this is usually a 7-day rotation.
Templates
Your rotation is composed of Day Templates. For example, a typical work day or school day might look something like this:
  • Midnight to 7:00am - Sleeping
  • 7:00am to 4:00pm - Getting ready, go to work or school, come home (non-discretionary time)
  • 4:00pm to 11:00pm - Discretionary time
  • 11:00pm to midnight - Sleeping
You customize your templates, and assign a template to each day of your rotation.
For example, somebody who works Monday through Friday might have a schedule / rotation that looks something like this:
Sun: Rest day, Mon: Work day, Tue: Work day, Wed: Work day, Thu: Work day, Fri: Work day, Sat: Rest day
