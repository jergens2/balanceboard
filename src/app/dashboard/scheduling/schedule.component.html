Rotation
Your rotation is the primary repeating cycle in your life.
Almost everyone spends their time in an arrangement which is rotational. For most people, this usually means arranging your time around your scheduled work or school hours.
In most cases, this is usually a 7-day rotation
By establishing your rotation here, you are simply designating all time into one of three classifications:
  • Time spent sleeping
  • Time spent doing non-discretionary activities
  • Time spent doing discretionary activities
Where, in this context, discretionary means "free time" to do whatever you please, and non-discretionary activities which, on a day-to-day basis, within the context of an established rotation, you don't have very much choice in how they are layed out.
